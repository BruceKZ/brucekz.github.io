# 学期计划 · Semester Plan

EPFL CS-251 Theory of Computation — Spring 2026 ([Moodle](https://moodle.epfl.ch/course/view.php?id=14459)).

## 课程结构 · Course structure

- **Part I — Finite Automata** (Lectures 1–3)
- **Part II — Computability** (Lectures 4–6 + Recap)
- **Part III — Complexity** (Lectures 7–12)

## 评分 · Grading

- **30%** — 3 homeworks, in groups of 2–4 students
- **70%** — Final exam (June/July)
